Education

Our facility helps with educating the youth and adults in the importance of saving and preserving animals and nature. We encourage our customers to learn from our experts in the importance of animal species and their environment. In hopes of saving animals especially those that are on the endangered list!

Wildlife Conservation

Our theme park was created to build revenue through our facility and donate it to charities. We created this theme park to make more income to donate to organizations that are non-profit in hopes of saving and preserving wildlife in its natural habitat.

WE ARE NOT A NON-PROFIT ORGANIZATION!

Covid-19

Here at our facility, we require all customers to wear a mask. This helps with the reduction in spreading infections of covid-19, especially those in the high-risk category. We sanitize every 15-30 minutes to ensure the safety of our customers. During this pandemic, our customer's and employee's health is one of our main priorities.
