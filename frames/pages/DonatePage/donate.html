VOLUNTEER FOR SUCCESS

Get involve in our park's conservation efforts. Our staff personnel will be happy to provide you with available opportunities in order for you to make a big difference in the preservation of our park's natural resources and general safety of the public. The opportunities range from a multitude of vital tasks necessary for the preservation of the park's natural resources.

Some of the activities included are: assistance for park tour guides, monitoring and assessments for park's current conditions, group's tasks ranging from the plantation of trees, litter collection, trails and road guard dispatch activities, phone operator, emergency radio relay assistance for tour groups, disaster notifications, veterinary assistance and monitoring of tour group's safety rules.

By providing your valuable time and efforts, you will help our staff personnel in their goal to ensure primarily the preservation of suitable natural habitat for the diverse fauna, furthermore by protecting the forest, it will support the global reduction of global Carbon footprint, allowing a cleaner environment for future generations to come.
